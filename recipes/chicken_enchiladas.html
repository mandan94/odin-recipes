Chicken Enchiladas

Descriptoin

Chicken enchiladas are packed with flavor and fantastic fresh, reheated or even bake from frozen. This recipe takes about an hour to prepare and 30 minutes to bake.

Ingredients

  • 12 medium size flour tortillas
  • 4 cooked chicken breasts
  • 2 cans of spanish rice
  • 1 can of refried beans
  • 1 can of rotel tomatoes
  • 2 cans of red enchilada sauce
  • 2 bags of mexican blend shreaded cheese
  • 1 bag of shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook chicken breasts
    2. They can be
    • Boiled
    • Baked
    • Grilled
    • Air-fried
  2. Chop chicken
  3. Mix rotel into chicken
  4. On each tortilla:
    • spoonful of spanish rice
    • spoonful of refried beans
    • spoonful of chicken/rotel
    • heaping pinch of mexican blend cheese
  5. Roll up tortillas and place side by side in baking pan
  6. Cover thoroughly with red enchilada sauce
    • Make sure red sauce completely covers tortillas and gets between each enchilada
      • If tortillas are left exposed they will burn
  7. Cover tops of enchiladas with mozzarella chese
  8. Bake pan at 350F for 30 minutes
