Corn Chowder

Descriptoin

Corn chowder is a quick and simple dish. It is savory, filling, and a meal all in itself. This recipe is for the instant pot, serves 8, and can be prepared in 15 minutes with a cooktime of an additional 15 mins. Keep in mind it can take the instant pot 30-45 minutes to build pressure.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes
  • 2 14.5 ounce cans of chicken broth
  • 3 cups of fresh or frozen corn
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Dice potatoes
  2. Chop onion
  3. Mince garlic
  4. Combine below ingredients in the instant pot and pressure cook for 15 minutes
    • potatoes
    • broth
    • corn
    • onion
    • garlic
    • salt
    • pepper
  5. Set instant pot to saute setting with low heat
  6. Stir in following ingredients for 6-8 minutes until thickened
    • cornstarch
    • cream
  7. Add cheese and continue to heat until cheese is melted
