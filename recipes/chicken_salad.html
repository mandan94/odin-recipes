Chicken Salad

Descriptoin

Chicken salad is fast and easy to make. It travels well and can be made into sandwiches or eaten with crackers. This recipe combines cold ingredients to make a light dish full of texture.

Ingredients

  • 3 cooked, boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 6 celery stalks
  • 3 large carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 cup of Helmans mayo
    • There is no acceptable substitue for Helmans mayo

Steps

  1. Cook chicken breasts
    2. They can be
    • Boiled
    • Baked
    • Grilled
    • Air-fried
  2. Chop chicken
  3. Chop celery
  4. Chop carrots
  5. Chop onion
  6. Mix all ingredients in a mixing bowl thoroughly
  7. (optional) Cover and place in the refrigerator for one hour
